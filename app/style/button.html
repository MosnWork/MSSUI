<button class="mss-button mss-button-big fn-mr10">基础按钮</button> <button class="mss-button fn-mr10 fn-bra5">圆角 fn-bra5</button> <button class="mss-button mss-button-small fn-mr10">基础按钮</button>

自选颜色背景默认全是灰色边框的，需要颜色到MSS预制里面选→

<button class="mss-button mss-button-big fn-mr10 fn-bra5 fn-bg-Red">大红，错误，危险</button> <button class="mss-button fn-mr10 fn-bra5 fn-bg-Orange">金黄，重要，警告</button> <button class="mss-button mss-button-small fn-bra5 fn-mr10 fn-bg-Green">绿色，成功</button> <button class="mss-button mss-button-small fn-bra5 fn-mr10 fn-bg-gay">灰色，失效</button>
